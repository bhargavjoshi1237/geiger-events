# Geiger Events — Why Switch

**Running your events on Eventbrite, Luma, Partiful, or a patchwork of tools? Geiger Events gives you all of it in one place — and treats you like the organiser, not the product.**

Geiger Events is a modern event platform for creators, communities, local businesses, and small conferences. You get a beautiful public event page and a branded organiser wall your fans can follow, plus ticketing with real depth: tiers, bundles, early-bird and dynamic pricing, discount codes, donations, group buying, memberships, and secure Stripe checkout — all as reusable pieces you set up once and attach to any event. Behind that sits a built-in CRM for your guests, multi-channel campaigns (email, SMS, WhatsApp, ads), no-code automations, and full day-of check-in from QR passes and wallet tickets to kiosk mode and multi-gate scanning.

Your attendees get an experience that makes you look good, too — a personal members portal where they manage tickets, orders, memberships, and refunds, show a scannable QR code at the door, and message you directly when they have a question.

It's **lighter, cheaper, and more social** than enterprise suites like Cvent or Bizzabo, and **far more capable and polished** than a basic RSVP or ticketing tool. No enterprise contracts, no "contact sales," no fighting hidden fees — just the power of a serious platform with pricing that makes sense for people who run their own events.

**Bring your next event over to Geiger Events — and run the whole thing from one place.**
